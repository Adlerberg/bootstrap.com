Rapid cost reduction

You don’t need to be under threat as a business to look at your cost base.

Too many organisations don’t for a variety of reasons; they don’t recognise the signs that they should, they have plenty of provision, they don’t have the expertise or the time, its not broken so don’t fix it

A review of your cost base should be as natural as putting on clothes in the morning and while you may be comfortable using your hard earned provision when times are good for whatever reason there are always imminent threats from new entrants to price wars to well, dare we say ….Brexit, that require a more rapid response and lets face it cost is within the control any organisation

bootstrap are masters of ‘the art of the possible’ without being paralysed by analysis and where rapid cost reduction is required we can implement ambitious but realistic plans at the pace needed, focused on tangible, high impact and sustainable results
