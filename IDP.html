Innovation Driven Procurement (IDP)

Traditional and transactional purchasing activity as a back office function is still largely tasked with the process of purchasing and unit cost savings. This position is changing largely in the Corporate environment with a greater focus placed on more strategic and value add procurement.

To achieve competitive advantage regardless of organisation size or market sector it is essential that agile procurement foundations are laid with different skill sets, people who can discuss and articulate more than just “cost”, able to collaborate more deeply with internal and external customers and manage the cost base in a different way; extending beyond unit cost and contracts, creating and extracting value to increase profit/EBIT and the overall attractiveness of the organisation to grow the customer base.

Either by identifying a test case or to transform your procurement model across your organisation wide bootstrap can drive and deliver the outcomes you need.
